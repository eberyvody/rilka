Background

Transcriptic gives scientists the power to design, run, and analyze cell and molecular biology experiments entirely from their laptops. Once experiments are submitted online, they’re executed remotely in Transcriptic’s fully automated robotic laboratories (or, "Workcells"). This software-defined infrastructure turns complex workflows from hours of mundane labwork into a matter of clicks, while massively reducing the upfront capital costs, lifting the focus from manual benchwork to the analytics and science.
